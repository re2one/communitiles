Introduction

Main Menu

This is where you land after logging in.

1) Shows the menu button. It lets you log out, navigate or quit an ongoing game.

2) Is where you can queue yourself to be paired when you're ready.

3) Leads you to the oversight of the game rules and the disclosure agreement, in case you want to study them further.

Pairing Screen

Clicking the Play Button takes you into this view. The screen remains unchanged until you eventually get paired.

4) You can cancel your pairing process to restart it if you feel stuck.

Start of a game

Before the game starts after you have been paired, you need to confirm that you are ready.

5) Hit the 'Go!' button to let your partner know you're ready. The Game will start after both players have hit the button and the Timer (13) will start to run. Better hurry!

Game Screen: Recommender

A recommending and a guessing Player play together. The recommender is the first one to move in a game. Each player has 20 seconds to move.

6) A segmented image is presented to the recommender. He has to reveal the whole image piece by piece to the guesser, who starts with an empty image. Selected segments turn fully visible, while the others stay opact to a certain degree.

7) The recommender has to reveal the segments in a manner, that the guesser can guess one of the labels presented to the recommender as quick as possible. Every unsuccessful guess decreases the amount of points (13) both players get assigned for the round.

8) The both players can see past guesses in this table.

9) After selecting a segment (6), the recommender can pitch it to the guesser by clicking the then enabled 'recommend' button.

We further enable the recommending player to accept a guess, if its really close or just typed wrong. Dont do this lightheartedly!

Game Screen: Guesser

After a pitch by the recommender, the guesser has three guesses for the label to be found.

10) Shows the already revealed segments.

11) To guess, the guesser has to enter his guess in the form field and submit it via the button. If he is completely clueless, he can request another segment via the 'show one more' button, although it will cost the remainder of guesses in points.

12) A popup notifies both players if the gamestate, which is also visible in the top bar, changes.

Further functions

12) Both players can quit a match if they find it too hard.

13) Shows the timer and the score elements.

Game End

14) the game ends if i) The guesser guesses a correct label, or submits a close enough guess that the recommender accepts, ii) One player logs out or quits the game or iii) The time runs out. The recommender will reveal up to 25 segments. After that, the whole image will be revealed and the guesser has three final guesses.

Points will be awarded only if the game is successfully finished.

After the game has ended with a positive or negative result, hitting the 'End Game' button will redirect you to the main menu. You can then hit the 'Play' button to start the next match.

Additional Advice

The configuration of the webserver will let a page refresh result in a 404 error. Please try to not refresh the app, you dont need to.

Use the built in controls to navigate within the app as the backwards button in the browser might lead to strange behaviour too.
